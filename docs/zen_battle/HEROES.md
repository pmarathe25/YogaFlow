# Heroes

Five heroes, each with 1 basic attack + 4 class skills + 1 Ultimate. Skills record offensive damage types where applicable: **PHY** (Physical) or **ELE** (Elemental). Pure heal/shield/buff skills have no damage type listed.

## Hero Stats

| Hero | Role | Element | HP | ATK | SPD | Unlock |
|------|------|---------|----|-----|-----|--------|
| Shanti (Calm) | Healer/Support | Water | 100 | 10 | 14 | Lv 1 |
| Santosha (Content) | Tank | Earth | 160 | 7 | 8 | Lv 2 |
| Virya (Vigor) | DPS/Brawler | Fire | 110 | 22 | 12 | Lv 3 |
| Dhairya (Courage) | Paladin/Buffer | Light | 135 | 15 | 10 | Lv 4 |
| Maitri (Loving-Kindness) | Mage/Healer | Air | 95 | 18 | 13 | Lv 5 |

- Level multiplier: +15% all stats per level
- Ultimate gauge: +20 on skill/Strike, +30 on Defend, costs 100
